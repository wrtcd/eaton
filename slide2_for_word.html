Column → mass (per grid cell)

σ = fp · ΔVCD   (molecules·cm−2)   [plume column: fp·ΔVCD from tempo_delta_vcd_plume]

A = ground area of one UTM 11N cell
A ≈ 1.04 × 107 m2
A in cm2 = A in m2 × 104

m = (σ · A in cm2) / (NA · MNO2)

NA = 6.02214076 × 1023 mol−1
MNO2 = 0.0460055 kg·mol−1

Optional — example (max-mass cell): σ ≈ 9.1 × 1016 molecules·cm−2
